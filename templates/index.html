What's New

Influence Explorer now shows regularly updated campaign finance data straight from the Federal Election Commission (FEC). As the 2012 elections heat up, you can be certain that you're getting up-to-date fundraising information for all federal candidates. (The FEC releases their data within one week of the candidate filing.)

The new data is available by selecting 2012 from the time dropdown on any federal politician page. You can follow fundraising in the presidential race by visiting the pages for Barack Obama, Mitt Romney or Newt Gingrich. Or search for any Senator or Representative in the search box at the top of the page.
