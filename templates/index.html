What's New

Influence Explorer now includes data on lobbyist bundling: lobbyist and lobbying firms that collect contributions from other donors on behalf of politicians. Through bundling, an donor who could normally only give a limited amount of money is able to present a candidate with far larger amounts of money. To get started, check out our overview of lobbyist bundling data, or look for the new Bundled Contributions table on politician pages, lobbyist pages and lobbying firm pages.
