What's New

Influence Explorer now includes two new datasets for tracking corporate influence in policy making. Our regulations section tracks companies' official public comments on federal rulemakings. You can use it to, for example, see what big drug companies are saying to the FDA. Our advisory committe section shows when employees of a company sit on federal advisory boards. Try looking up the advisory roles of employees of big banks.

There's also a new section on environmental enforcement actions from the EPA. Lots of big companies have received hefty fines from the EPA.
